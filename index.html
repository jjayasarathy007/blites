Your Questions Answered

What makes BLITES shirts special?

Our shirts are made from the finest cotton and silk blends, offering unparalleled comfort and a luxurious feel. Each piece is designed for durability and lasting elegance.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Delivery times vary based on destination. Please check our shipping policy for more details.

How should I care for my shirts?

We recommend dry cleaning or a gentle cold wash cycle for our shirts to maintain their quality and shape. Avoid harsh detergents and high heat.

How do I find my correct size?

Our sizing is designed to be true to size. Please refer to our detailed size guide on the website for precise measurements to find your perfect fit.

What is your return policy?

We have a 30-day return policy for unworn items in their original condition. Please visit our returns page for instructions on how to initiate a return.
